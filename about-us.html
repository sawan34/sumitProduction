Sumit Productions started by SUMIT SAHNI

An entrepreneur who is based in Delhi and personifies an eccentric and inspiring spirit; untamed yet sophisticated, spontaneous yet focussed, cosmopolitan with a zest of life; enthusiastic and tremendously visual.

His journey with the art form began 14 years ago when he was just 13 years old and without any formal training, his undying passion and commitment had led him start his venture ‘Sumit Productions’ , a lot has changed since then from a one man business to a full service office staff and production team that has been successful in capturing 2000+ events.

Sumit Productions is a full fledge production house that caters all the digital photography & cinematography needs. It is a one-stop shop for capturing all events from social to commercial, from Big fat Weddings, Pre-wedding shoots, Destination shoots in India & Overseas to IIFA 2016, Movie Launch events, MIJWAN Show by Manish Malhotra, Advertisments & many more.

Our Philosophy is not only to capture a perfect picture rather we ensure to seize the perfect moment and promise to gel our artistic talent with the latest high definition technology which produces best results that will amaze you!!

It is a place, where you find brilliant photography in unusual style & professionals dedicated in turning dream into reality.
